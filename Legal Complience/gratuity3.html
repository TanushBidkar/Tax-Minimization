Gratuity Calculator

What is Gratuity?

Gratuity is a monetary benefit provided by an employer to an employee as a token of appreciation for their long and meritorious service. It is typically paid when an employee completes a minimum of 5 years of continuous service with the same employer.

Eligibility Criteria for Gratuity Payment

Must have completed a minimum of 5 years of continuous service with the same employer.

Gratuity is payable to employees in both the private and public sectors.

It is paid upon retirement, resignation, or death.
